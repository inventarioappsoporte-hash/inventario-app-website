Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2025

1. Information We Collect

At Inventory App, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it, in compliance with Google Play Store policies.

1.1 Information you provide directly

  • Inventory data: Product information, prices, stock, categories, and movements you enter
  • Images: Product photos you take or select from your gallery
  • Authentication data: Security PIN and biometric data (stored locally only)
  • Contact information: Email when you contact us for support
  • Settings: App preferences, language, theme, alerts

1.2 Automatically collected information

  • Usage data: Features used, session time, navigation patterns
  • Device information: Model, operating system, app version, unique device identifier
  • Performance data: Error logs, crashes, and performance metrics
  • Camera data: Temporary access for barcode scanning (no images stored)
  • Purchase information: In-app purchase history (PRO version)

1.3 Sensitive data and device permissions

Our application requires the following permissions to function properly:

  • Camera: To scan product barcodes
  • Storage: To save inventory data and export reports
  • Internet: Only for cloud backups (PRO version) and in-app purchases
  • Biometric authentication: To protect app access (PRO version)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the application service
  • Improve functionality and user experience
  • Provide technical support when you request it
  • Send important updates about the application
  • Detect and prevent technical problems

3. Data Storage

3.1 Local Storage

All your inventory data is stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • Product information
  • Stock movements
  • Custom categories
  • Application settings

3.2 Cloud Backups (Optional)

If you activate the cloud backup function (only available in PRO version):

  • Data is encrypted before being sent to Google Drive
  • Only you have access to these backups
  • You can deactivate this function at any time
  • Backups are stored in your personal Google Drive account

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • When required by law or competent authorities
  • To protect our legal rights or the safety of other users
  • With service providers who help us operate the application (under strict confidentiality agreements)

5. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Authentication with PIN and biometrics (PRO version)
  • Secure local storage on your device
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to data by our team

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can access all your data from the application
  • Rectification: You can modify or correct your information at any time
  • Deletion: You can delete all your data from the app settings
  • Portability: You can export your data in CSV format
  • Opposition: You can deactivate optional features like cloud backups

6.1 Data retention and deletion

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary:

  • Local data: Remains on your device until you delete it manually
  • Cloud backups: Retained according to your settings (you can delete them anytime)
  • Support data: Deleted 30 days after resolving your inquiry
  • Error logs: Retained for a maximum of 12 months for technical improvements
  • Purchase data: Retained according to Google Play policies (minimum 3 years)
  • Analytics data: Aggregated and anonymized after 24 months

7. Google Play Store Compliance

This application complies with all Google Play Store policies, including:

  • User Data Policy: We handle data transparently and securely
  • Families Policy: Content appropriate for all ages
  • Permissions Policy: We only request permissions necessary for functionality
  • Payments Policy: Clear and secure transactions through Google Play Billing

7.1 Required vs optional data

Data required for basic functionality:

  • Product information (name, price, stock)
  • Basic app settings

Optional data (you can choose not to provide):

  • Product photos
  • Cloud backups
  • Biometric authentication
  • Contact data for support

7.2 Data handling transparency

We commit to:

  • Being transparent about what data we collect and why
  • Obtaining your consent before collecting sensitive data
  • Allowing you to control and delete your data at any time
  • Not selling or renting your personal information to third parties
  • Notifying you about any changes in our data practices

8. Third-Party Services

Our application may integrate the following third-party services:

8.1 Google Play Services

  • Google Play Billing: To process in-app purchases
  • Google Drive API: For cloud backups (PRO version only)
  • Google Play Core: For updates and distribution

8.2 Third-party policies

These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Terms of Service

Important: We have no control over the privacy practices of these third-party services. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

9. Regional Rights

9.1 European Union (GDPR)

If you reside in the EU, you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to be forgotten: You can request complete deletion of your data
  • Portability: You can export your data in a readable format
  • Rectification: You can correct inaccurate data
  • Processing limitation: You can restrict how we use your data

9.2 California (CCPA)

If you reside in California, you have rights under CCPA:

  • Right to know what personal information we collect
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising these rights

9.3 Other countries

We respect local data protection laws in all countries where we operate and provide similar protections to all our users.

10. Children's Privacy Protection

Important: Our application is not specifically directed at children under 13, but may be used by them under parental supervision for educational purposes (learning about inventory management).

10.1 Commitment to child protection

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • We do not use advertising targeted at minors
  • We do not share data from minors with third parties
  • Parents can request deletion of their children's data

10.2 Recommendations for parents

  • Supervise the use of the application by minors
  • Review the data their children enter
  • Set up parental controls on the device
  • Contact us if they have concerns about usage

If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13 without verifiable parental consent, we will take immediate steps to delete such information.
