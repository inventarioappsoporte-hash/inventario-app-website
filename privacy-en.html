Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

At Inventory App, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1.1 Information you provide directly

  • Product data you enter in the application
  • Contact information when you write to us
  • Application settings and preferences

1.2 Automatically collected information

  • Application usage data (features used, usage time)
  • Device information (model, operating system, app version)
  • Error logs to improve the application

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the application service
  • Improve functionality and user experience
  • Provide technical support when you request it
  • Send important updates about the application
  • Detect and prevent technical problems

3. Data Storage

3.1 Local Storage

All your inventory data is stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • Product information
  • Stock movements
  • Custom categories
  • Application settings

3.2 Cloud Backups (Optional)

If you activate the cloud backup function (only available in PRO version):

  • Data is encrypted before being sent to Google Drive
  • Only you have access to these backups
  • You can deactivate this function at any time
  • Backups are stored in your personal Google Drive account

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • When required by law or competent authorities
  • To protect our legal rights or the safety of other users
  • With service providers who help us operate the application (under strict confidentiality agreements)

5. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Authentication with PIN and biometrics (PRO version)
  • Secure local storage on your device
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to data by our team

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can access all your data from the application
  • Rectification: You can modify or correct your information at any time
  • Deletion: You can delete all your data from the app settings
  • Portability: You can export your data in CSV format
  • Opposition: You can deactivate optional features like cloud backups
